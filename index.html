About

Fotis Zagas is a professional photographer based in Thessaloniki, Greece, specializing in architectural photography, drone sessions, immersive 3D virtual tours, and video production. With a meticulous eye for composition and light, every project becomes a visual narrative that reveals the soul of a space.

Bridging technical precision with artistic vision, the work goes beyond simple documentation — it transforms properties and environments into compelling stories that resonate with audiences and elevate brands.
